## Packages
framer-motion | For smooth page transitions and micro-interactions
recharts | For visualizing job stats and earnings
react-dropzone | For handling file uploads (job proofs)
date-fns | For formatting dates nicely

## Notes
- Images for job proofs will be handled as data URLs for MVP simplicity since no S3 bucket is configured.
- Razorpay integration is mocked for the demo.
- Authentication is session-based via standard cookies.
- "Before", "After", and "Disposal" photos are required for job completion.
